The Beatles

The Beatles were an English rock band, formed in Liverpool in 1960. With members John Lennon, Paul McCartney, George Harrison and Ringo Starr, they became widely regarded as the foremost and most influential act of the rock era. Known as the Fab Four, they made a name for themselves due to their inspiring lyrics and instruemntals. I dicovered The Beatles when I started to venture and change my music interests to rock. My parents were always fond of them and this relfected on my childhood. Since I was not alive during the time they were playing, I live through the remastered versions of their songs on Spotify, and Youtube.
