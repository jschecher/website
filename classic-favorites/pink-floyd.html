Pink Floyd

Pink Floyd was an English rock band originating in London. Their progressive and psychedelic music caught the attention of people all over the world and their success sky rocketed through their talented lyrics and vocals. Pink Floyd is one of the most commercially successful and influential groups in the history of popular music. I have never listene to Pink Floyd until my sister introduced me to them through Spotify. The second I heard their song, Money, I became hooked and added them to my playlist instantly. I have always seen their famous symbol of their album cover "Dark Side of the Moon", but not until recently didn't understand how amazing they truly were. They are an all time classic favorite that will never go out of style or popularity.
