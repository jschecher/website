"Views" Drake

Drake recently released an album called "Views" and it is striking the Billboard Charts and sales. When I first listened to this album, I appreciated the vision he had and the catchy beats he used which strays away from his usual slow songs. Since Drake is known for releasing a lot of music at once, it was not a surprise to see 23 songs on the album. All of them are unique in their own right, and hold a distinct message throughout the lyrics. I have a few favorite songs on this album, but also a few I am not too fond of. Overall, I think he produced good quality music that represented his hometown, Toronto.
