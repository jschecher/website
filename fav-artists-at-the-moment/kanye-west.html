Kanye West

Among being a wife to Kim Kardashian, father, and public figure, Kanye West began rapping at a very young age and has evolved into the talk of the rap industry ever since he showed the world his talents. He is known for more than just rapping, but making very public opinons on a variety of issues. Lately, I have been listening to his new album, Life of Pablo. This album graced Billboard's charts and caused contraversy due to the racey lyrics that is displayed.

 

About Kanye West

Kanye West is a singer/songwriter/rapper who was born and raised in Chicago. He has never been shy through his music, and is constantly expressing himself through his raps and lyrics. He is one of the best selling artist of all time, selling over 32 million albums since he has started his career. Winning a total of 21 Grammys and gracing mulitple "Greatest of All Time" lists, including Forbes. He has proven to be an influential person on and off the stage, in more ways than one, including some negative influences as well. Aside from his strong opinions, his music stays true to his talent and his ablities.
